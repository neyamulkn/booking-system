What You Need to Know About AppointMantic for Your Massage Therapy Business

Online appointment booking has never been easier. With AppointMantic you can run a stress-free massage therapy business with a jam packed calendar, 24/7 and all year round. You can schedule appointments, assign tasks and receive payments in a complete transparent process.

What is a massage therapy appointment booking software?

Running a massage therapy business is hectic which requires you to be on top of marketing, staffing, operations, and more. On top of that you need to concentrate on bringing in more clientele for that much needed income. A massage therapy appointment booking software helps you deal with the operation part effortlessly by allowing your customers to book appointments online, reschedule or cancel appointments efficiently. Now you don’t need to spend hours on the phone to receive calls for all these. Your AppointMantic page will take care of the whole process of booking appointments online.

Will using a massage therapy software help increase sales?

We can’t guarantee sales for your business but using AppointMantic software will help you to become efficient in your massage therapy practice, and let you concentrate on winning clients.

Moreover, with AppointMantic you can reduce the chances of no-shows and cancellations, and instead schedule more customers instead of wasting your entire day waiting on them.

Do I need to have tech knowledge to use AppointMantic?

Not at all! With its easy-to-use and intuitive design, AppointMantic booking software is extremely simple to set up. All you have to do is fill in your massage therapy business details, add your services, and staff members just like you would on a computer. Then make it live for customers to access.

How will customers know about my massage therapy practice on AppointMantic?

You can share your business page URL on social media and other marketing channels to promote online appointment scheduling. You can also connect your business page to your website to let customers know they can start booking appointments online.

Do you store my customer details in your database?

To keep a record of appointments booked and allow customers to access to your calendar, we retain your customer details online so that you may access it via our dashboard. Your customer details are confidential and we will never share it with anyone.

How will I know when I get a massage therapy appointment from a customer?

When you get a booking online, you will be notified of the booking with customer details. You can then choose to assign staff member for the massage therapy session or you can choose to work on the appointment yourself.
